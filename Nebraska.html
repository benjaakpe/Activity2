Nebraska - some refer to it as the "beef state", other know it because of the corn grown here, but some folks also barely hear about this state. I have lived here for a few years now and I have learned to enjoy the peace and quiet this place offers, as compared to larger metropolitan areas. The crime rate is relatively low and so is the cost of life here.

One of the most exciting things about this state is the fact that it host the College World Series every year, except of 2020 due to the ongoing pandemic. This event usually attracts hundreds of thousand fans of baseball from all corners of the United States. The picture below was taken during the 2019 kick off of the College World Series.
